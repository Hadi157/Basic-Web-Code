Nana Komatsu

Komatsu was born in Tokyo and brought up in Yamanashi. She started modeling at 12. She has been a House Ambassador for Chanel since 2015.Nana started her career as a model for Chatmonchy's video clip in the song Concenience Honeymoon in 2012. Even in his first appearance in a film called The World of Kanako, he immediately won the award for best new artist.

Review

The film, starring Nana Komatsu, Mugi Kadawaki, and Ryou Narita tells the story of friendship between Leo (Nana) and Haru (Mugi). They suddenly became popular thanks to their very good Indie music. They perform music on the street. Things got a little more complicated when they decided to take a tour of the area. There is a love triangle between them. Leo who has feelings for his manager Shima (Narita), but Shima has feelings for Haru. They decided to disband while they were on the rise, with their last tour.
